# Commercial Scenario Engine

## Product boundary
Decision support tool for mid-to-large e-commerce brands with meaningful paid media spend,
repeat-customer dynamics, and multi-product catalogues.

Not for: lead generation, offline-heavy, marketplace-only, low-SKU, or enterprise CDP-native businesses.

Full definition: `docs/product-boundary.md`

## MVP purpose
Prove that bounded commercial headroom can be modelled credibly enough to improve client conversation.

## Out of scope
**Product**
- Lead generation, offline-heavy, marketplace-only, low-SKU businesses
- Enterprise / warehouse-native / CDP-native organisations
- Formal vendor recommendation
- Precise financial forecasting

**Engineering**
- Auth, database, saved sessions
- Features or abstractions not explicitly described
