# Product Boundary

Commercial Scenario Engine is a bounded decision support tool for mid-to-large e-commerce brands with meaningful digital revenue, meaningful paid media spend, broad product inventory, and repeat-customer dynamics.

It estimates plausible commercial headroom from first-party data activation, existing customer suppression, experimentation, and related interventions over a 12-month horizon.

It is intended for situations where the business is trying to understand how far current or near-current capabilities can be extended before more substantial customer data, activation, or operating-model change becomes warranted.

## In scope
- Retail / e-commerce brands
- Repeat-purchase dynamics
- Paid media as a meaningful lever
- Multi-product catalogue
- Pre-major-stack-choice or pre-CDP commitment

## Out of scope
- Lead generation businesses
- Offline-heavy businesses
- Marketplace-only sellers
- Low-SKU direct response businesses
- Highly mature warehouse-native or enterprise CDP-native organisations
- Formal vendor recommendation
- Precise financial forecasting

## MVP purpose
Prove that bounded commercial headroom can be modelled credibly enough to improve client conversation.
